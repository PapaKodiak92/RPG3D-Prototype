# Lesson 01: The First Scene

## What we are building

A tiny 3D world with:

- A ground area
- A player placeholder
- A camera

This is the first step before movement, trees, inventory, combat, or multiplayer.

---

## Coding idea

This lesson teaches that games are made out of small parts.

In Godot, those parts are called **nodes**.

A scene is a group of nodes saved together.

---

## Files we will create later

```text
client/
  project.godot
  scenes/
    world/
      StarterWorld.tscn
    player/
      Player.tscn
```

---

## What to test

Press Play and confirm:

- The scene opens.
- The ground is visible.
- The player placeholder is visible.
- The camera can see the scene.

---

## Kid challenge

Change the size of the ground.

Questions:

- What happens if the ground is bigger?
- What happens if the player starts somewhere else?
- What happens if the camera is too close?
